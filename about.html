About Original Shunmugam Snuff & Co.

A Heritage Passed Through Five Generations

Today, the company is owned and managed by Mr. Sukumar Shanmugam Mudaliar, representing the fifth generation of this remarkable lineage. What began as a small hereditary loom-based business has flourished into a globally recognized snuff manufacturing house rooted in tradition, authenticity, and uncompromised quality.

With a proud legacy dating back to 1839, Original Shunmugam Snuff & Co. has built a name that stands for consistency, craftsmanship, and deep cultural heritage. Over generations, we have remained committed to preserving traditional methods while meeting the expectations of modern markets.

Global Reach Across Continents

Our products have reached customers across the world, including the United States, UAE, Europe, Sri Lanka, China, Bangladesh, Nepal, and several other regions. With a strong export network and a trusted distribution system, we continue to deliver premium snuff to both Indian and international markets.

Pioneers & Preferred Choice of Many Legends

Over the decades, several renowned leaders, cultural icons, and film personalities from South India have been loyal users of our snuff. Their trust stands as a testament to the purity, aroma, and consistency that define our products.

Our Iconic Brand – World Brand Shunmugam Snuff (Ulaga Mark)

Among our range of offerings, the flagship product — World Brand Shunmugam Snuff (Ulaga Mark) — remains our most celebrated and widely recognised brand. Loved for its distinctive blend and superior quality, it continues to be the heartbeat of our company.

Crafted with Tradition. Perfected with Expertise.

Each batch of snuff is carefully processed using time-honoured techniques, premium ingredients, and strict quality control. Our manufacturing practices blend heritage with hygiene, ensuring that customers receive the finest, most authentic snuff experience.

Trusted Across India & Beyond

From local retailers to global distributors, we supply to numerous parts of India and international markets, maintaining long-standing partnerships built on reliability and excellence.

A Legacy That Continues to Grow

As we step into a new era with the fifth generation leading the journey, our mission remains unchanged — to honour our heritage, uphold our quality, and continue serving generations of loyal customers worldwide.
